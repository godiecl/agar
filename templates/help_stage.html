Stage 1: Ambiguity Assessment

In this stage, you will individually evaluate each software requirement to determine its level of ambiguity. Please carefully choose from the following options:

  • No Ambiguity: The requirement is clear and has only one interpretation.
  • Low: Minimal ambiguity, generally clear but could be slightly improved.
  • Medium: Noticeable ambiguity, needs clarification.
  • High: Very ambiguous, difficult to interpret clearly.

Please evaluate each requirement carefully before proceeding.

{% elif stage == 2 %}
Stage 2: Clarification and Improvement

In this stage, the system displays software requirements where potentially ambiguous terms have been automatically detected and highlighted in different colors to assist your review. You are expected to clarify these terms by replacing them with more precise expressions or by rewriting the sentence, if necessary.

After reviewing and editing the requirement, click the Save button to store your changes. Once saved, the Next button will be enabled so that you can proceed to the next requirement.

Important: If you make any additional changes after clicking Save, you must click Save again before continuing. This ensures that only finalized edits are submitted.

If needed, you may rewrite the entire requirement to ensure clarity.
